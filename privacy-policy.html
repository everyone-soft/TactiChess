TactiChess Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 5, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to TactiChess. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any information when you use our chess application.

Information We Collect

TactiChess does not collect any personal information. Our app functions completely offline and does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your location
  • Use analytics services
  • Contain advertisements
  • Make network requests
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other device data

App Functionality

TactiChess is a chess training application that provides tactical puzzles and AI gameplay. All functionality is processed locally on your device. Your game progress and settings are stored only on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special provisions for children's data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Third-Party Services

TactiChess does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or SDKs that would collect user data.

Data Storage

Any app data (such as game progress or settings) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers. This data is managed by iOS and can be removed by uninstalling the application.
